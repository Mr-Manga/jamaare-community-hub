NEPA (Electricity Services)

NEPA (Ayyukan Lantarki)

https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1581091870622-1e7e8f9f2c7b
How to Pay Bills:
Visit the nearest NEPA office or use mobile banking apps that support NEPA payments.
Yadda ake Biyan Kudin Wuta:
Je zuwa ofishin NEPA mafi kusa ko amfani da manhajojin banki na waya.
Report Faults:
Call the local NEPA fault line or visit the office in Jama’are to report outages or issues.
Bayar da Rahoton Matsala:
Kira layin NEPA ko je ofishin NEPA a Jama'are.
Consumer Rights:
You have the right to fair billing, timely repairs, and safe electricity supply.
Hakkin Mai Amfani:
Kana da hakkin samun adalci wajen biyan kudin wuta da gyara cikin lokaci.
Emergency Contact:
NEPA Jama’are Office: 0802 345 6789
Lambar Gaggawa:
Ofishin NEPA Jama'are: 0802 345 6789
