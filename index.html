Community Development Project

Building a brighter future for Jama'are through education, health, and sustainable services.

Schools

Providing access to quality primary, secondary, and tertiary institutions in Jama'are.

  • Government Day Secondary School Jama'are
  • Jama'are Model Primary School
  • College of Education Jama'are

Hospitals

Ensuring accessible healthcare services for the people of Jama'are.

  • General Hospital Jama'are
  • Primary Health Care Center Jama'are
  • Private Clinics and Pharmacies

NEPA (Electricity Supply)

Providing reliable electricity supply to support homes, schools, and businesses in Jama'are.

NEPA Office Location: Near Jama'are Central Market, Bauchi State.
